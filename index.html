prithvi cafe

i had visited in dec 2018

Prithvi Cafe is a nice and cozy casual dining cafe in Juhu with soothing interiors and a homely touch. Its outdoor seating is very vibrant and you have the option of dining under trees, close to nature. This restaurant is best known for quick snacking, Coffee and Fast Food that is served here at really economical and budget-friendly prices
